# Executive Guide: Natal Chart

## 1. Basic Structure

The natal chart is a map of the sky at the exact moment of your birth. It's divided into:

```
                         MC (Midheaven)
                              ↑
                         House 10
                    House 11    House 9
               House 12              House 8

    AC (Ascendant) ←  ●  CENTER  ●  → DC (Descendant)

               House 1               House 7
                    House 2     House 6
                         House 3
                              ↓
                         IC (Imum Coeli)
```

---

## 2. The 4 Main Axes

| Axis | Symbol | Meaning |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| **AC** (Ascendant) | House 1 | Your social mask, first impression, appearance |
| **DC** (Descendant) | House 7 | Relationships, partner, what you seek in others |
| **MC** (Medium Coeli) | House 10 | Career, vocation, public image |
| **IC** (Imum Coeli) | House 4 | Home, roots, family, private life |

---

| **11** | Friends, groups, goals, ideals |
| **12** | Unconscious, spirituality, isolation |

### Elements (colors in chart):
- **Fire** (red): Energy, action, passion - Aries, Leo, Sagittarius
- **Earth** (green): Practicality, stability - Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn
- **Air** (blue): Intellect, communication - Gemini, Libra, Aquarius
- **Water** (purple): Emotions, intuition - Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces

| Symbol | Planet | Represents |
|---------|---------|------------|

| ☽ | **Moon** | Emotions, instinct, mother |

| ♃ | **Jupiter** | Expansion, luck, philosophy |
| ♄ | **Saturn** | Structure, limits, karma |
| ♅ | **Uranus** | Revolution, originality |

**Retrograde (R)**: When a planet appears to move "backwards". Its energy becomes more internal/reflective.

---

## 6. Aspects (lines between planets)

| Aspect | Angle | Color in chart | Meaning |
|---------|--------|----------------|-------------|
| **Conjunction** | 0° | Gold | Energy fusion (intense) |
| **Opposition** | 180° | Solid red | Tension, polarity |
| **Trine** | 120° | Solid blue | Harmony, flow (positive) |
| **Square** | 90° | Dotted red | Friction, challenge |
| **Sextile** | 60° | Dotted green | Opportunity (soft positive) |

---

## 7. Quick Chart Reading

1. **Sun** → Your essence (in which sign and house)
2. **Moon** → Your emotions (in which sign and house)
3. **Ascendant** → How you're perceived (which sign is in House 1)
4. **Planets in houses** → Where that energy manifests
5. **Aspects** → How energies interact with each other

---

## 8. Practical Example

If you see in the chart:
- ☉ in ♑ House 10 → Sun in Capricorn in House 10 = Career-oriented, ambitious person
- ☽ in ♋ House 4 → Moon in Cancer in House 4 = Very connected to home and family
- Blue line between ☉ and ☽ → Trine Sun-Moon = Harmony between identity and emotions
